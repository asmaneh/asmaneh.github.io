برگزاری سخنرانی مجازی: «تحقیقات باستان‌شناسانه در بوستان ناصیف (بعلبک): نگاهی اجمالی به تاریخ شهر از دورهٔ باستان متأخر تا دوره‌‌ٔ مملوک»

 

سخنران:  هایک لمان، والنتینا وتزولی

میزبان: مؤسسۀ فرانسوی شرق نزدیک (Ifpo)

زمان: ۸ ژوئيه ۲۰۲۱| ۱۷ تیر ۱۴۰۰

 

▪️Archaeological Research in Bustan Nassif (Baalbek): A Glimpse into Urban History from Late Antique to Mamluk Period

Heike Lehmann, Valentina Vezzoli

 

This lecture presents the results of architectural and archeological research carried out in Baalbek by the Deutsches Archäologisches Institut, the BTU-Cottbus and the General Directorate of Antiquities of Lebanon and focused on the understanding of the urban development of the site. In particular, architectural and archaeological records of the excavated area of « Bustan Nassif », which covers the time span from the late Roman to the Mamluk period, will be presented and contextualized with the overall urban development of Baalbek. These data will be compared with the abundant material evidence collected in this area. A significant quantity of ceramics has been unearthed, documenting the history of common and fine wares on the site from the 12th through the early 15th centuries. Archaeological data from this sector have also provided evidence for the understanding of an assemblage of sphero-conical vessels.
