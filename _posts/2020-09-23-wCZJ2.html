▪️ARQUEOLÓGICA 2.0 & GEORES – 2021 invites authors to submit original and unpublished work in applied and innovative digital heritage areas such as climate change hazards, monitoring and mitigations, collaborative environments, documentation of cultural heritage, high-end digitisation, 3D modelling and informative models of objects, monuments and sites, artificial intelligence, deep learning, big data, digital twins, life cycle management, virtual archaeology, virtual conservation/restoration, virtual museums, virtual exhibitions, cultural heritage gaming, innovative e-learning, and internet technologies and social media in cyberarchaeology.

 

▫️THEMATIC AREAS

 

- Digitization and documentation of cultural heritage: data acquisition with metric, photogrammetric, remote sensing and geophysical devices

- High-end digitization and 3D modelling and informative models (as HBIM, GIS) of objects, monuments and sites

- Virtual Archaeology/Virtual Architecture/Virtual Museums/Virtual Exhibitions

- Cultural Heritage Gaming

- Internet Technologies and Social Media in Archaeology

- EXtended/Virtual/Augmented Reality (XR/VR/AR) and Digital Twins: Virtual Conservation/Restoration

- Artificial Intelligence (AI), Deep Learning (DL), Big Data, and 5G

- Semantics, Object Libraries, Catalogues and Vocabularies

- Copyright, IPR (Intellectual Property Rights) and LOD (Linked Open Data), including GOD (Geographic Open Data)

- Collaborative Environments for Cultural Heritage, including CDE (Common Data Environment)

- Inspections and NDT (nondestructive testing): remote control, on-site robotizations (RPAS and others)

- Life Cycle Management and Planned Maintenance, Preventive Maintenance

- Heritage Urban Landscape (BIM-GIS Integration) and Cross-fertilization into Urban Districts

- Climate Change Hazards, Monitoring and Mitigations

- Digital Twins: Scalarization of Heritage Complexity Surveying Praxis, Monitoring and Maintenance Programmes

- Education and Innovative E-learning

- Public Archaeology and Cyberarchaeology
